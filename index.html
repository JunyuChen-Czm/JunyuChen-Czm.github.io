CMDS-AD: Cross-Modal Dual-Stream Decoupling for Few-Shot Anomaly Detection

CMDS-AD: Cross-Modal Dual-Stream Decoupling for Few-Shot Anomaly Detection

Junhao Cai, Deyu Zeng, Junhao Pang, Junyu Chen, Qiwei Liang, Xiaopin Zhong, Zongze Wu

Junhao Cai, Deyu Zeng, Junhao Pang, Junyu Chen, Qiwei Liang, Xiaopin Zhong, Zongze Wu

European Conference on Computer Vision (ECCV), 2026

该工作面向小样本异常检测场景，提出跨模态双流解耦框架，结合 RGB 与 3D 几何信息缓解训练样本稀缺带来的性能瓶颈， 并通过更精细的跨模态对齐与噪声抑制提升异常检测效果。

This work targets few-shot anomaly detection by proposing a cross-modal dual-stream decoupling framework that leverages both RGB cues and 3D geometric information to overcome severe data scarcity while improving cross-modal alignment and anomaly localization.

Featured Project ECCV 2026
CMDS-AD: Cross-Modal Dual-Stream Decoupling for Few-Shot Anomaly Detection CMDS-AD: Cross-Modal Dual-Stream Decoupling for Few-Shot Anomaly Detection

这是一个面向小样本异常检测的跨模态工作，结合 RGB 与 3D 几何信息，通过双流解耦设计、 跨模态语义对齐与噪声抑制机制，提升极少样本条件下的异常检测性能。

This project focuses on few-shot anomaly detection by leveraging both RGB and 3D geometric cues. It introduces a dual-stream decoupling design, cross-modal semantic alignment, and noise suppression to improve anomaly detection under extremely limited data settings.

作者：Junhao Cai, Deyu Zeng, Junhao Pang, Junyu Chen, Qiwei Liang, Xiaopin Zhong, Zongze Wu
